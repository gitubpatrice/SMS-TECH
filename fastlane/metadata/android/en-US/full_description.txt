SMS Tech is an ad-free, tracker-free SMS / MMS app for Android. It replaces the stock messaging app while keeping every message on your device — no cloud, no analytics, no third-party SDK shipping your data anywhere.

Features
========

• SMS and MMS — full default-SMS-app support on Android 8 (API 26) through 15+
• Voice messages — push-to-talk recording, sent as a real MMS (compatible with every recipient that has MMS)
• Encrypted vault — hide sensitive conversations behind SQLCipher + Android Keystore. Toggle "Lock vault on leave" to re-lock the vault every time the app goes to background
• Panic code — a second unlock PIN that opens the app in a decoy view where the vault stays invisible and inaccessible
• Biometric unlock — fingerprint or face, with a PIN fallback you set yourself
• Contextual reply — long-press a message, pick "Reply" and your composer shows a mini-quote so the recipient sees what you are answering
• On-device translation — translate any message to your locale using Google ML Kit models cached locally. No text is ever sent to any server
• OS-wide blocked numbers — at first launch, SMS Tech mirrors the system blocklist (Phone app, Samsung Messages) and retroactively deletes the conversations that match
• Attachment picker — Photo, Video, File, Contact card. Photos are automatically re-encoded as JPEG to fit MMS carrier caps
• Full-text search across every conversation, with FTS4 indexing
• Export a conversation as a PDF for archival
• Encrypted local backup using a passphrase distinct from your unlock PIN
• Themes — Light / Dark / AMOLED / "Dark Tech" slate-blue accent
• French and English UI
• Confirmation toggle in Settings → Send → "Confirm before send" lets you require a confirmation dialog before any SMS, MMS or attachment is dispatched

Privacy
=======

• No analytics, no crash reporters, no third-party tracking SDK
• Only network usage: your carrier MMSC (for MMS transport) and Google ML Kit servers (one-time, on first translation, to download the language model)
