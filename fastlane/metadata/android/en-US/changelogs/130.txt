v1.24.0 — Important security fix + fixes

• The local database was encrypted with an incorrect key
  in every earlier version. It is rebuilt with the correct
  key on first launch, without losing a message. Details
  in SECURITY.md.
• Very long conversations open and scroll much faster;
  the app also starts up faster.
• Fixed: a conversation's preview stayed stuck on a
  deleted message.
• 100% local, no data leaves your device.
