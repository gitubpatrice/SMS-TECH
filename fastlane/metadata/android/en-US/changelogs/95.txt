v1.15.1 — Scheduled messages (new feature)

• Schedule an SMS for later: type your
  message, then ⋮ → "Schedule…" → pick
  a date and time. The SMS is sent
  automatically at that time, even if
  the app is closed.
• Manage scheduled messages from Settings
  → "Scheduled messages": see the list of
  pending SMS, cancel any of them.

Infrastructure existed since v1.5 but
had no UI surface — now fully usable.
