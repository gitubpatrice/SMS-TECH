v1.15.2 — Backup restore (new feature)

• Restore an encrypted .smsbk backup:
  Settings → Backup → "Pick a .smsbk" →
  enter the original passphrase →
  conversations + messages merged.
• Existing messages kept (no duplicates
  via unique index).
• Atomic transaction: a crash mid-restore
  rolls back everything.
• Passphrase eye toggle on both backup
  and restore dialogs.

Closes the backup story (export + restore).
