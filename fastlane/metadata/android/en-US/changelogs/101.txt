v1.18.0 — Thread screen refactor

• The conversation thread's message item
  renderer (~140 lines of inline audio /
  media / text bubble dispatch) is now
  extracted into a dedicated
  `ThreadMessageItem` composable with a
  `@Stable` actions bundle.
• Recompositions are better scoped: an
  item re-composes on its own message
  changes, not on the parent's global
  state. Smoother scrolling on long
  threads.
• No user-visible change.
