v1.3.1 — Notify the recipient of your emoji reactions

WHAT'S NEW
* When you post an emoji reaction on a received message, a SMS containing only the emoji is sent to the sender (and only to them, never to the entire group). The recipient knows you reacted.
* Settings > Sending > "Send my reactions to recipient" toggle. ON by default, confirmation dialog at the very first send with "Don't ask again" checkbox.

SECURITY & RELIABILITY (12 audit fixes)
* Strict refusal of non-dialable recipients (banks, INFO, premium short codes) to avoid billing surprises.
* 60 s dedup window on tap bursts (anti-spam on null > emoji > null > emoji hesitation).
* Anti-race between the confirm dialog and concurrent reaction changes.
* "Don't ask again" persisted only after a successful send.
* UI defense-in-depth: "React" button hidden on your own outgoing messages.
* Single target: only the sender of the message receives, never the other group participants.
* No signature appended to the emoji (avoids multi-part billing).
* Dialog autofocuses on "Cancel" + Checkbox a11y grouping (TalkBack).
