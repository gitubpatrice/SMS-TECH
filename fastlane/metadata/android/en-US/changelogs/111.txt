v1.22.0 — Dual SIM

• Fix: on a dual-SIM phone, MMS received on the
  secondary card (e.g. a foreign SIM) could
  arrive late or not at all. They are now
  downloaded through the correct SIM.
• New: the SIM that received each message is
  shown next to the contact's name (e.g.
  "Marie · SIM 2"), only when two SIMs are
  active.
• 100% local, no change to your data.
