v1.18.2 — Fix back-button crash (One UI 8 / Android 16)

• Fixes a crash that could happen when pressing
  "back", which appeared after the June 2026
  One UI / Android update.
• Cause: a navigation-library bug tied to the
  animated back gesture (predictive back),
  triggered by the new system. The animated back
  gesture is turned off until the library is
  fixed — back works normally.
• No data, encryption or feature change.
  100% local.
