v1.14.3 — Hotfix migration emergency notif

Fix for dirty state post-v1.14.0/v1.14.1: if you
had disabled emergency mode before v1.14.2, the
lock-screen notif reappeared at each app launch
regardless.

One-shot migration at startup: if emergency
mode is disabled but the notif shortcut flags
are still ON → force-clear automatically
(idempotent, 1 time per cold-start).

Combined with v1.14.2: all accidental SMS
trigger paths + residual dirty state are
cleaned up.
